About Us

KFC (Kentucky Fried Chicken) is a world-renowned fast-food restaurant chain known for its delicious fried chicken. Founded in 1952 by Colonel Harland Sanders, KFC has become one of the most iconic and beloved fast-food brands globally.

At KFC, we are committed to serving our customers with mouthwatering, crispy fried chicken that's cooked to perfection. Our secret blend of 11 herbs and spices has been a closely guarded recipe for generations, delivering that unique and irresistible flavor you know and love.

Our mission is to provide a memorable dining experience with our finger-licking good chicken, a variety of sides, and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're dining in or ordering takeout, we're here to satisfy your cravings.
